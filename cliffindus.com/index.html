About

Built for the businesses
that run your neighborhood

"Local businesses deserve the same quality of technology that large chains have — not templates, but complete platforms."

Cliff Industries builds software for local and regional businesses. We focus on retail, automotive, hospitality, and operations — the kinds of businesses that are the backbone of every community but rarely get enterprise-quality tools.

Every product is built end-to-end: the mobile app, the web dashboard, the backend API, the database, and the cloud infrastructure. No handoffs, no gaps.

Based in Texas, USA. Named for the cliff and the Indus — elevation and flow, structure and ambition.
