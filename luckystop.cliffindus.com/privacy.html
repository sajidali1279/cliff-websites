Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 2026  ·  Version 1.0

1. Who We Are

Lucky Stop Rewards is a loyalty platform operated by Cliff Industries on behalf of Lucky Stop convenience store locations throughout the United States. Cliff Industries is the data controller for purposes of this Privacy Policy.

2. What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to anyone who uses the Lucky Stop Rewards mobile app or the associated web admin portal at admin.luckystop.cliffindus.com — including customers, employees, store managers, and administrators.

3. Information We Collect

When you register

  • Phone number — used to verify your identity via OTP and as your account identifier
  • Full name — displayed on your account and transaction records
  • Date of birth — used to verify minimum age (18+) requirements

When you use the app

  • Transaction history — purchases, credit balances, and redemptions
  • Location (optional) — used to show your nearest Lucky Stop location; only collected while the app is open and only if you grant permission
  • Receipt photos — uploaded by employees to verify transactions; stored securely
  • Device token — used to send you push notifications

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your loyalty account and calculate cashback credits
  • To process and verify in-store transactions
  • To send you push notifications about offers, order status, and account activity
  • To show relevant store locations near you
  • To detect and prevent fraud
  • To generate store-level analytics for store managers and administrators

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Lucky Stop store operators — store managers can see transaction history for their location
  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and push notifications
  • Cloudinary — secure storage for receipt photos and promotional images
  • Render / Neon — backend hosting and database infrastructure

All third-party providers are contractually required to process data only as directed by us and to maintain appropriate security standards.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information is deleted within 30 days. Transaction records are anonymised and retained for billing integrity purposes.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Deletion — delete your account at any time via Profile → Delete My Account
  • Opt out of notifications — disable push notifications in your device settings at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Lucky Stop Rewards is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account promptly.

9. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Phone numbers and sensitive fields are encrypted at rest. Receipt photos are stored in a private cloud bucket not accessible to the public. We conduct periodic security reviews of our infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and notify active users via push notification or in-app message if the changes are material.
